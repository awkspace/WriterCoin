    Welcome to WriterCoin.

    For years, if not decades, writers have struggled with attaching a value to the written word. WriterCoin changes all that by introducing a currency specifically for writers.

    Designed from the ground up to be innovative, creative, and art-loving, WriterCoin will change the way you look at all forms of the written word, from Aristotle's Republic to dirty Sonic the Hedgehog fanfiction.

    Powered by art.

    Hate the idea of making real, dirty, commercialized money from your writing? We do too. Writing shouldn't be about trading in your soul for money â€” it should be about trading art for art. WriterCoin aims to facilitate that trade as an art-centric currency.

    Art has inherent value and power that our economy of greed cannot touch. That's why WriterCoin's motto is Verpa Fortiorem: "The Pen is Mightier." With WriterCoin, we as writers can float above the day-to-day grind and focus on consuming and creating new art.

    By writers. For writers.

    Unlike other cryptocurrencies that are based on complicated math problems nobody cares about, WriterCoin is earned by using that liberal arts degree you always insisted would come in handy. For every ten words of prose you write, you receive one WriterCoin.

    Don't write on a computer? Later this month, WriterCoin will be launching a new wifi-powered miner block that attaches to virtually any typewriter. Now you aren't just writing, you're mining.
